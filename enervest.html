Delivering Net Zero

We're committed to funding projects that directly contribute to carbon neutrality by 2050. Every project on our platform is vetted for its potential climate impact.

A Three-Pillar Approach

Our net-zero strategy is built on three key pillars that guide our project selection and community engagement:

  • Renewable Energy Expansion

    Funding solar, wind, hydro and other clean energy projects to displace fossil fuels

  • Energy Efficiency

    Supporting projects that reduce energy consumption in buildings and industry

  • Carbon Removal

    Investing in nature-based solutions and direct air capture technologies

About Enervest

We're on a mission to democratize access to renewable energy funding while delivering measurable climate impact.

Founded in 2020, Enervest has become Indonesia's leading renewable energy crowdfunding platform. We connect passionate individuals and organizations with impactful clean energy projects that need funding.
